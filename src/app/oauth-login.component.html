This is a demo of oAuth Login with 3rd Party Identity Provider (IdP), LinkedIn in this case. Security and privacy is paramount important to you (and "US") and you are in complete control of authorization. Most importanly, you NEVER give your user/password to anyone other than the Identity Provider (i.e. LinkedIn) as you will see in this demo. I am using LinkedIn for demo purpose but I would be using Custom Identity Provider (implementation of Ping Federate) at the enterprise environment. The oAuth flow (Authorization Code) that is used here is unique and you may not find elsewhere on the internet. The actual authentication/authorization flow starts at the server-side and Angular has NO information about oauth2 clientid and/or client secrete! Like this pattern? Follow me on LinkedIn.
